Running / Jogging

Equipment:

Running shoes

Benefits:

Running is one of the most effective forms of aerobic exercise. It can improve heart health, burn fat and calories, and lift your mood.

Walking daily can reduce your risk of heart disease, obesity, diabetes, high blood pressure, and depression.

Safety:

Walk in well-lit and populated areas. Choose shoes that offer good ankle support to reduce your risk for injury.

Duration and Frequency:

Running: 20 to 30 minutes, 2 to 3 times per week

Walking: 150 minutes per week, or 30 minutes 5 days a week

If You're a Beginner:

Run for 20 to 30 minutes twice a week at a conversational pace. Alternate between 5 minutes of running and 1 minute of walking. Always stretch after your run.

For walking, aim for 150 minutes per week. Break this down into 30 minutes a day for 5 days or shorter 10-minute sessions three times a day.

Use a fitness tracker to monitor steps. Start with your base and gradually increase by 500 to 1,000 steps every 1 to 2 weeks.
