Skating

Equipment:

Roller skates, Figure Skating Boots, Safety helmet, Knee guard

Benefits:

Skating improves cardiovascular fitness, works your lower body and core, and provides an effective cardio workout at home.

Safety:

Skate during daylight hours, skate with a friend if possible, and always wear protective gear. Avoid skating in traffic, and stay on the right side of sidewalks or trails when passing others.

Duration and Frequency:

30 to 45 minutes, 3 to 4 times per week

If You're a Beginner:

Start with basic training by wearing your skates at home to get comfortable with the movement. Practice in a safe, clear area before moving to more challenging environments.
